Are you an investor?

We would love to have you join 175+ firms including 500 Startups, Andreeson Horowitz, Greylock, Sequoia, in #MovingForward.

To join the community, we require the following:

  1. Publicly available anti-harassment/discrimination policies
  2. Point(s)-of-contact for entrepreneurs to ask questions and report issues
  3. A short statement on their efforts to nurture diverse, inclusive and harassment-free workplaces

Any firm can fill out this form at any time and we'll get you onboarded. There is also an option to pledge your commitment, with the goal to fully submit within 30 days.

We have provided the following guides to get you started and help your process. For a more comprehensive list of resources, please visit our Media page.
