Policy Directory

There is an inherent power inbalance between VCs and founders. While employees are protected by anti-harassment and anti-discrimination laws, founders do not fall under the category of "employee" when fundraising from a VC firm. This puts founders in a vulnerable state if they experience any form of harassment / discrimination.

#MovingForward facilitates VC firms in writing external anti-harassment and anti-discrimination policies, as well as providing reporting channels. With each firm that joins our movement, we hope to spark conversations internally and also support founders in reviewing policies, finding supportive investors, and identifying contacts.

Our goals for this initiative are:

  1. Launch a public, collective step forward for the venture industry
  2. Facilitate transparent reporting channels
  3. Provide a universal, open-source, and evolving wiki for continuous updates
