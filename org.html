Our Organization

Origins

In 2017, a question came up at a founder's dinner.

"As an entrepreneur, if I experience harassment or discrimination, where do I report it and how will I be protected?"

No one had a good answer and so four entrepreneurs came together to solve this issue. The following year, #MovingForward was born with 39 VCs committed to the movement upon launch.
