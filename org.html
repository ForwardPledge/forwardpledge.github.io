Our Organization
Origins

In 2017, a question came up at a founder's dinner:

"As an entrepreneur, if I experience harassment or discrimination, where do I report it and how will I be protected?"

No one had a good answer and so four entrepreneurs came together to solve this issue. The following year, our organization was born with 39 VC firms committed our mission upon launch.

#MovingForward is a nonprofit organization dedicated to increasing transparency and accountability of venture capital firms around diveresity and inclusion. We have worked to foster a community of investors who are committed to moving this industry forward by publishing external anti-harassment and anti-discrimination policies.

Our Work
Directory

In 2018, we launched a directory of diversity, inclusion, and anti-harassment commitments with almost 40 venture capital firms including 500 Startups, Sequoia, and Andreessen Horowitz. Our open-source directory now has policies from 175 firms over 23 different countries.

Newsletter

Our monthly newsletter, Steps by #MovingForward, gives a quick rundown of the state of tech culture. Every month we highlight all-stars working to improve culture and diverisity in venture capital. Our goal is to spark conversations, ask tough questions, and encourage everyone to take a step forward.
